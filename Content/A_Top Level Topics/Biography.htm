Biography

Riley King was born in Itta Benna, Mississippi in 1925. Like many blues players, B.B's music allowed him to break free from a hardworking, impoverished life in the rural South. Over the course of his continuing career, B.B. has produced over sixty albums and performed over 300 concerts annually. B.B's guitar skills, musical style, and dazzling performances have propelled him to legend status in the blues community. Along with his contemporaries, B.B. has influenced countless musicians including Buddy Guy, Eric Clapton, Jeff Beck, and Stevie Ray Vaughan.

B.B. died in 1925.
